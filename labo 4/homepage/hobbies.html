Judo

Judo is a modern Japanese martial art and combat sport that was developed in the late 19th century by Jigoro Kano. The word "Judo" can be translated as "gentle way," which reflects its fundamental philosophy and techniques. Judo is known for its emphasis on using an opponent's force and balance against them rather than relying solely on one's own strength

Tennis

Tennis is a popular racket sport played by two or four players (singles or doubles) on a rectangular court divided by a net. It is both a recreational activity and a competitive sport with a rich history.
